# Business Flow

## Overview

The Inventory Management System follows a simple workflow to manage products and inventory efficiently.

The system starts with supplier registration, followed by product creation, inventory updates, and purchase order processing.

---

## Workflow

```text
Supplier Registration
        │
        ▼
Product Creation
        │
        ▼
Inventory Update
        │
        ▼
Purchase Order
        │
        ▼
Inventory Reports
```

---

## Step 1 – Supplier Registration

The administrator adds supplier information into the system.

Example:

- Supplier ID
- Supplier Name
- Phone Number
- Email

---

## Step 2 – Product Creation

Each product is linked to a supplier.

Example:

- Product ID
- Product Name
- Category
- Price

---

## Step 3 – Inventory Management

Inventory is updated whenever:

- New stock arrives
- Products are issued
- Stock quantity changes

---

## Step 4 – Purchase Orders

Purchase orders are created whenever stock needs replenishment.

Purchase Order contains:

- PO Number
- Product
- Quantity
- Status

---

## Step 5 – Reports

The system generates reports for:

- Available Stock
- Supplier Details
- Purchase Orders
- Low Stock Products

---
